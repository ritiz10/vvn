About Our School

Our History

Founded in 1992, VETRI VIVEKANANDA has been providing quality education for over 33 years. We started with just 30 students and have grown to become one of the most respected primary schools in the area.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing environment where children can develop intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically through a balanced curriculum and caring teachers.

Our Values

  • Respect for all
  • Love of learning
  • Creativity and curiosity
  • Community spirit
  • Personal excellence
